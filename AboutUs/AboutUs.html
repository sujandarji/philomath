About This Website

'Philomath | Sujan Darji - Official Website' was developed by Mr. Sujan Darji alone by using HTML, CSS, JavaScript and PHP programming languages. The word "Philomath" means- someone who loves learning new things. This site was started in 2021 AD as a simple one-person internet site with an original intension to post mostly educational materials in the context of Nepal. Now, we provide book reviews, educational informations, guidance and latest discovery news, and revision notes for students from various streams through this website. We are dedicated to providing you the very authentic educational content through this website, with an emphasis on concepts and ideas to tackle easily with the various problems that appear during student life.
